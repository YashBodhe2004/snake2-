INSTRUCTIONS:-

Objective:
Eat berries , grow the snake, and avoid collisions.

Controls:
Use arrow keys to navigate.

Gameplay:
Move the snake with arrow keys or WASD.
Eat food to grow longer.
Avoid walls and your tail.

Scoring:
Each food eaten adds points.

Game Over:
Hit walls or your tail.

Tips:
Plan moves ahead.
Be strategic for high scores.
Enjoy the Snake Game! Good luck!
